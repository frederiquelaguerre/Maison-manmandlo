Welcome to our artisan vacation home, right on the beach of Saint-Pierre bay, at the foot of Mount Pelée.
Rebuilt after the 1902 eruption, it blends historical charm with modern comfort. Step onto the beach straight from your door, and end each day with a spectacular sunset from the terrace.

This one-of-a-kind home is the perfect setting for a relaxing, unforgettable stay — whether with friends or family.

  • Ground floor: Step directly onto the beach for peaceful moments with your feet in the water. A convenient wet room for rinsing off after a swim. An outdoor table on the sand for brunch with a view of the crystal-clear bay.
  • Upper floor: A bright, open living space with views of both the sea and the mountain. Open-plan kitchen and lounge opening onto a large terrace — perfect for dinner at sunset.
  • Three air-conditioned bedrooms:
    • Master: sea view with en-suite bathroom
    • Mountain-side: two single beds with private shower room
    • Sea-side mezzanine: double bed with a stunning view

Amenities

Everything you need for a perfect stay

The house sleeps up to 8 guests and is fully equipped for a comfortable, hassle-free holiday.

Guest access

You have access to the entire house except for one storage room on the ground floor. Check-in and check-out are fully self-managed thanks to our secure key box.

Location & activities

Ideally located to explore the northern Caribbean coast:

  • Shops within walking distance (mini-market 5 min away)
  • Saint-Pierre's museums and diving clubs just steps away
  • Wild black sand beaches 20 min away (Anse Céron, Anse Couleuvre)
  • Carbet waterfalls 15 min away
  • Mount Pelée hiking trail 20 min away (starting from Morne Rouge)

Saint-Pierre is about 50 km north of Fort-de-France — a 45 to 60-minute drive along the N2 (Caribbean coast road). Renting a car is the best way to explore the area at your own pace. Seasonal ferry services also run between Fort-de-France and Saint-Pierre in around 30 minutes.
Yes, there is a dedicated parking space right in front of the house. Saint-Pierre is a relaxed town where seafront parking is easy and free.
